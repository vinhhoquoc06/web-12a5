We are one!

High school is always a very important period in a student's life. 3 years of high school is a time for each young person to accumulate knowledge, ready to enter the tough battle to enter the dream university. This is also the time when each student has happy and innocent childhood memories with friends. The best stories about the final high school students below are the innocent emotions, the most sincere voices of those girls and boys.
Every time summer comes, phoenix flowers bloom red in the streets as well as in the school yard, it is also the time when many young people have to say goodbye to their beloved school to continue entering a new learning environment. The day of saying goodbye to school, saying goodbye to senior friends who studied together, sharing joys and sorrows in life will certainly evoke a lot of emotions in the hearts of teenage girls and boys. So, on this day, please send the most sincere and meaningful goodbyes to your classmates who have been with you during your time in school!
